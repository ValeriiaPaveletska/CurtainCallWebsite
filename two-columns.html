FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Curtain Call free?

Curtain Call is an app designed to build communities. For optimised customer experience, we do not have advertisements or pop-ups in our app. Therefore, to make it sustainable and optimal for both our users and our team, the app has a … one-time payment fee. Our website, however, is completely free to use!

Can I buy tickets directly through Curtain Call?

We provide you with all the information needed and with the links to the official theatre-pages where you can purchase the tickets.

Does Curtain Call organise the plays it advertises?

Although Curtain Call does sponsor or collaborate with some of them, our brand does not organise any of the events we advertise.

I accidentally swiped left on the play I’m interested in, what do I do?

To see previously viewed plays in the Curtain Call app you can go to your profile screen, then to settings, then to history. Keep in mind that you can only view the last 20 events you viewed.

Do I get a refund if the show is cancelled?

Curtain Call redirects you to an official page, therefore you do not buy tickets directly from us. We would recommend contacting the venue for a refund.

I’m getting harassed in one of the communities, what do I do?

Curtain Call believes in equal treatment and respectful communication. We have moderators available in each community to remove people that go against our policy. If you think that it’s taking our moderators too long, you can contact them privately or send a message to our support group.

Are Curtain Call events wheelchair accessible?

Unfortunately, not all venues are able to accommodate people with disabilities or in need of additional support, therefore we make sure to state in the description of every event/play whether it is accessible or not. Contact our support group if the information on our platform is incorrect.

How can I join the Curtain Call team?

To join the Curtain Call team you can contact our email curtaincallhr@gmail.com.
