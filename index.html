What is Curtain Call?

Curtain Call is an app developed and produced by theatre lovers for theatre lovers!

Using the popular Tinder-like gamification, we turned searching for plays in your area into a game that will help you connect with fellow theatre lovers!

It's just that simple! Match with plays, chat with people, and most importantly, have fun!

Our vision

Do you remember that excursion with the school to the theater? The smell of fresh popcorn when your parents took you to the movies. Those are unforgettable aspects of these experiences. We want to recreate this feeling and give everyone an opportunity to relive their childhood memories through the joy of art and performance.

Our mission

To create a platform that makes connection for theatre lovers more accessible throughout the Netherlands.

Our values

The magic of the curtain rising

The excitement and anticipation that comes with the moment the curtain rises symbolizes everything Curtain Call stands for. Our brand is all about creating shared experiences. Theater is something you experience together. Just as the opening of the curtain signals the beginning of a new story, Curtain Call offers a fresh, modern way to experience theater - together with others. We create a platform where theater lovers can find each other, talk about performances, and enjoy all that theater has to offer together.

New faces in the spotlight

We believe in honoring tradition while embracing innovation. We want to make theater accessible because we believe that people should be able to freely share emotions and experiences without any difficulty. Innovation is a steppingstone in our brand and we want to create a place where people can easily engage and enjoy theater with easier access to various theater-related events.

The old smell of velvet seats

We can feel a lot of things only once, that's why remembering some of them is so comforting. Same goes with the sense of smell, that first time your parents took you to the theater and that old and dusty, yet charming smell of the seats hit your nose. Everyone is different, yet, we have sensations and experiences that relate to us all.

The echo of applause

Applause is a common way amongst many cultures to display appreciation for something. At Curtain Call we appreciate and celebrate theatre and the connections it brings. Not only do we honor the art of theatre itself, but also the people who make it possible together with the audience. We foster a space where every standing ovation keeps the spirit of theater alive.
